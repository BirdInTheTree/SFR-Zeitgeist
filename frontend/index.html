SRF Zeitgeist

HEADLINES:

SRF Zeitgeist shows what Switzerland discussed today — derived from SRF news subtitles, not clicks.

An LLM segments each broadcast into editorial stories, merges them across programs, and ranks by five signals: novelty (spike vs baseline), spread (how many desks covered it), persistence (how often it returned), prominence (total airtime), and primetime presence.

Each image is a video frame from the story's most important moment. Click any cell to see source quotes and links to Play SRF.
