About this demo

SRF Zeitgeist ranks topics from SRF news subtitles by measuring which phrases spike versus the previous week, then cleans the result with deduplication and an LLM quality gate.

The goal is to show a topic-first entry point into the archive: what Switzerland discussed today, with quotes and links back to Play SRF.
